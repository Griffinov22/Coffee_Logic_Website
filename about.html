About Us

Coffee Logic is a sustainable and modern take on coffee. Driven by a desire to serve the people, we bring delicious espresso with a purpose on: one cup at a time. And it all starts in the mountains of Colombia where good things grow.

Coffee Logic is a new type of coffee experience that brings together quality, sustainability, and community. Our focus is on creating an environment that nurtures people's minds, hearts and bodies through our vision of continuous improvement in education, production and differentiation through sustainable agriculture.
